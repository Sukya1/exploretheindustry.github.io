A Hackathon is an event, usually sponsored by tech companies and organisations, where programmers get together for short periods of time to collaborate on a project. There are often awards and prizes awareded to groups with impressive and/or impactful projects.

Here are a few Hackathons hosted at Penn:
  • Femmehacks

Career Fairs are a great way to find summer internships and get to meet recruiters from various companies. There are usually a number of career fairs happening on campus during recruiting season, August - Feburary. To be notified of these fairs ensure that you have set uo your Handshake account and selected computer science as one of your areas of interest.
