A Hackathon is an event, usually sponsored by tech companies and organisations, where programmers get together for short periods of time to collaborate on a project. There are often awards and prizes awareded to groups with impressive and/or impactful projects.

Career Fairs are a great way to find summer internships and get to meet recruiters from various companies. There are usually a number of career fairs happening on campus during recruiting season, August - Feburary. To be notified of these fairs ensure that you have set uo your Handshake account and selected computer science as one of your areas of interest.

How do I get an Internship?

Searching

Handshake, Github, Linkedin and Career Fairs are great places to search for Internship opportunities. See what companies your friends and classmates have worked for, they may be able to help you get a referral.

See Popular List
Interviewing

Leetcode, Hackerrank, Algoexpert and Cracking the Coding interview are great platforms to practise interview-like questions. You can prepare using mock interviews on Pramp and Interview.io

Accepting the Offer

Before accepting an offer, ensure that the salary meets your basic needs and negotiate the offer, if necessay. Remember, it is unethical to renege an opportunity if a better offer is presented to you.
